# dXb Tech Inc. - Project Executive Summary
**Date:** March 2026
**Target:** National Research Council of Canada Industrial Research Assistance Program (NRC IRAP) - AI Assist Stream
**Project Name:** Hasti AI (Etsyseolab-6)

## 1. Company Overview
dXb Tech Inc. is a Canadian technology startup based in Quebec, dedicated to bridging the gap between independent artisans (SMBs) and advanced e-commerce intelligence. Our flagship product, **Hasti AI**, is a highly specialized AI Co-pilot designed to democratize search engine optimization (SEO) and market intelligence for global e-commerce platforms, starting with Etsy.

## 2. The Innovation (Technical Uncertainty & AI Integration)
The current ecosystem of e-commerce SEO tools relies on static keyword scraping and retrospective data analysis. These tools are incapable of understanding the nuanced, unstructured data of niche markets (e.g., jewelry, vintage) or dynamically adapting to platform-specific algorithmic shifts.

**Hasti AI** introduces significant technical innovation through:
- **Generative AI & Deep Learning Integration:** We are not merely wrapping an off-the-shelf LLM. We are building a proprietary "Deep Fetch" architecture that ingests unstructured product data, historic sales velocity, and platform API signals, feeding them into a specialized AI engine. 
- **Contextual Ranking Algorithms:** Developing algorithms that evaluate "Product Health" dynamically (A+ to C- grading) based on real-time multi-variable analysis (character limits, tag optimization, material specificity).
- **The Technical Challenge:** Structuring high-volume, chaotic API data from diverse artisan shops and training the AI to autonomously generate compliant, high-converting metadata without human intervention. Overcoming the token-limit and latency challenges associated with real-time batch processing (Vision-powered batch fixes).

## 3. The Need for Support (CTO & Scaling)
We have successfully developed a robust Minimum Viable Product (MVP) capable of real-time store scanning, automated Shopify migration, and bilingual (FR/EN) operations. We have demonstrated early traction via a growing, active waitlist and live Stripe integration. 

To transition from MVP to a globally scalable, enterprise-grade AI architecture, dXb Tech Inc. requires specialized technical leadership. Funding from the **IRAP AI Assist Stream** will be directly allocated to:
1.  **Onboarding a dedicated Chief Technology Officer (CTO)** with deep expertise in scalable cloud architecture and machine learning pipelines.
2.  **Expanding our R&D team** to refine our proprietary ranking algorithms and data-ingestion models.
3.  **Covering computational costs** associated with training and deploying our specialized AI models.

## 4. Canadian Economic Benefit
This project will generate high-value, tech-focused employment in Canada. Furthermore, Hasti AI will directly empower Canadian artisans and small businesses to compete on the global stage, increasing their export revenue by optimizing their digital storefronts for international discovery.

## 5. Financial Capability & Co-funding
dXb Tech Inc. is positioned to meet the 25% co-funding requirement through a combination of sweat equity (management labor), bootstrapped infrastructure investment, and projected early-stage revenue streams derived from our verified waitlist conversions upon full launch.
