# dXb Tech Inc. - Business Model & Traction Report
**Project:** Hasti AI (Etsyseolab-6) | **Date:** March 2026

## 1. Value Proposition
Etsy sellers spend an average of 10-15 hours a week managing SEO and analyzing competitors. Hasti AI reduces this to minutes, acting as an automated, AI-powered Co-pilot. We specifically target high-margin niches like Jewelry, Vintage, and Handmade Crafts.

## 2. Monetization Strategy (SaaS)
We operate on a freemium SaaS model processed securely via **Stripe**.
- **Starter Tier (Free):** Basic store health scan, limited to 5 product optimizations per month.
- **Pro Tier ($19/mo):** Unlimited AI optimizations, Competitor Radar, automated Shopify export (CSV), and seasonal keyword trends.
- **Enterprise/Agency ($49/mo):** Multi-store management, dedicated API limits, and 1-on-1 strategy calls.

## 3. Market Traction & Go-To-Market (GTM)
- **Waitlist Generation:** Our optimized landing page is currently capturing active leads directly into our Supabase database. These represent our initial cohort of beta testers and early adopters.
- **GTM Strategy:** Leveraging existing Etsy seller communities on Facebook/Reddit, utilizing targeted bilingual (FR/EN) marketing in Canada, and demonstrating the product via TikTok/Reels "before & after" case studies.

## 4. Competitive Advantage
Unlike generic SEO tools (e.g., Marmalead, eRank) that only provide keyword data, Hasti AI **executes** the changes. We don't just show the seller what is wrong; our AI rewrites the title, generates the 13 optimal tags, and pushes the update directly to the Etsy store via OAuth 2.0.
